Product

Features & Specifications

Our product is a robotic support suit for lower limb rehabilitation. Stroke patients and patients suffering from partial paraplegia are treated with therapy for gait correction and limb rehabilitation. Robotics for motor function rehabilitation is an emerging new field of engineering and is expected to grow exponentially over the next couple of years. Our project consists of a framework that will be worn by the user over his lower limb system. The frame would contain indents for two motors at hip level. Motor application would be controlled by the user on a joystick via a microprocessor. The exoskeleton depends partly on the programming and partly on the mechanical design. A walker would be provided for balance on which the controllers would be placed.
